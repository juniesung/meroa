# Meroa — Support

Need help with Meroa? You're in the right place.

## Contact us

The fastest way to reach us is by email:

**meroa.app@gmail.com**

We read every message. We can't promise a specific response time, but including your
device type (iPhone / Android), the app version, and a short description of what happened
helps us help you faster.

## Common questions

**How do I delete my account?**
Open Meroa → **You** tab → **Delete account**. Deletion is immediate and permanent. You can
also delete your account on the web at [WEB DELETION URL] — no app required.

**How do I export my data?**
Open Meroa → **You** tab → **Export my data**. You'll get a file containing your account
data.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Subscriptions are billed by the App Store or Google Play. Cancel in your **App Store** or
**Google Play** account settings. Note: deleting your Meroa account does **not** cancel a
store subscription — cancel it in the store to stop billing.

**Is Meroa a real person?**
No. Meroa is an AI companion and is always identified as AI. It is **not** a therapist,
doctor, or financial adviser, and does not give professional advice. If you're in crisis or
have an emergency, please contact your local emergency services or a qualified
professional.

**How do I report an offensive AI response?**
In the chat, open the options on the AI message you want to report and choose **Report this
response**. You can add an optional reason. We review reports to improve the service.

**How is my data used?**
See our [Privacy Policy]([PRIVACY URL]). In short: your messages are sent to a third-party
AI service to generate replies (with your consent), and we don't sell your data or track
you across other apps.
