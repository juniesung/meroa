# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 20, 2026
**Last updated:** July 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Meroa ("Meroa", "we", "us") collects,
how we use it, and the choices you have. Meroa is a relationship-first AI companion
app. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use Meroa.

**Who we are.** Meroa is operated by Jun Kwon,
California. You can reach us at meroa.app@gmail.com.

---

## The short version

- We collect your **phone number** (to sign you in), the **messages** you send Meroa,
  and the **tasks, goals, and memories** you create in the app.
- Your **messages are sent to a third-party AI service** to generate replies. We ask for
  your explicit permission before this happens, and you can withdraw it at any time.
- We **do not** use advertising, we **do not** sell your data, and we **do not** track you
  across other apps or websites.
- You can **export** your data and **delete your account** — a deletion permanently and
  immediately removes your data from our systems.

---

## 1. Information we collect

We only collect information you give us by using the app. We collect:

- **Phone number.** Used as your account identity and to send one-time login codes.
- **Your messages.** The messages you send Meroa and the replies Meroa generates.
- **Your content.** Tasks, goals, progress entries, and records you create.
- **Memories.** Facts Meroa remembers to personalize your experience (for example, a
  preference or a goal you mentioned). You can view, edit, mark sensitive, suppress, or
  delete these at any time.
- **Preferences.** Settings such as your chosen chat style, quiet hours, timezone, and your
  AI data-sharing consent.
- **Subscription status.** Whether you have an active subscription (verified through the
  app store and our payments processor).
- **Diagnostics.** Technical error reports, used to find and fix crashes and bugs.

**What we do not collect.** We do **not** collect location, contacts, photos, your
microphone or camera, device advertising identifiers, or any product-analytics tracking.
We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

## 2. How we use your information

- To provide the core service: hold a conversation, create and update your tasks and goals,
  and remember relevant context over time.
- To sign you in and keep you signed in securely.
- To generate AI replies (see Section 3).
- To manage your subscription and verify entitlement.
- To keep the app reliable (diagnosing errors and crashes).

We do **not** use your information for advertising, and we do **not** sell it.

## 3. AI processing and third-party sharing

Meroa's replies are generated by a **third-party AI service**. When you send a message,
the **content of that message** — together with information about your tasks and goals
needed to respond — is transmitted over an encrypted connection (HTTPS) to that service to
produce a reply. We do **not** send your phone number or account identifier to the AI
service.

**Your consent is required.** Before any of your messages are sent to the AI service, we
ask for your explicit permission inside the app. **You can withdraw this permission at any
time** in Settings; if you do, chat will stop working until you grant it again, because the
feature cannot function without it.

We share information with a small number of service providers, only as needed to run Meroa:

| Provider | What it receives | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Our AI provider (a third-party AI service) | The content of your messages and related task/goal context | To generate replies |
| Our payments provider and the app stores (Apple / Google) | Your subscription status and an account identifier | To manage subscriptions |
| Our error-monitoring provider | Technical error reports | To keep the app working |
| Our hosting provider | Hosts our servers and database | Infrastructure |

We do not otherwise share, rent, or sell your personal information.

## 4. How your information is protected

Information is **encrypted in transit** (HTTPS) and **encrypted at rest** in our database.
Login codes and session tokens are stored **hashed**, not in plain text.

## 5. How long we keep it

We keep your information for as long as your account exists. **When you delete your
account, we permanently and immediately delete your data** from our systems (see Section 6).
Login codes are short-lived and expire on their own.

Deleting your account removes your data from Meroa; it does **not** cancel a subscription
purchased through the App Store or Google Play. To stop billing, cancel the subscription in
the App Store or Google Play.

## 6. Your rights and choices

- **Access / export.** You can export a copy of your data from within the app.
- **Correction / deletion of memories.** You can edit, suppress, or delete individual
  memories, and tell Meroa not to bring something up.
- **Account deletion.** You can delete your account and all associated data from within the
  app, or via our web deletion page at [WEB DELETION URL]. Deletion is immediate and
  permanent.
- **Withdraw AI consent.** You can withdraw permission for AI processing at any time in
  Settings.
